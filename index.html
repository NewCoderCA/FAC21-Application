On this is journey to join Founders and Coders, they ask why should you be chosen.

Well I say why not Me?

I am a Black female, Black mother, Black sister, wife, cousin, aunt and lover. Black friend, team member, work colleague, team player and co-worker. I am a Black coder, leader, Black learner, aspiring techy developer and problem solver. I am a Black female communicator, collaborator, excited to create new ideas and see them come to life. I am a Black female who likes to laugh and giggle but mostly enjoys the sun who comes with a diverse perspective and likes to get things done. Because of all I am, I say why not choose me.


Why not Founders and Coders?
