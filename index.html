Why not Me?

I am a Black female, Black mother, Black sister, wife, cousin, aunt and lover. Black friend, team lead co-ordinator, work colleague and team player. I am a Black coder, leader, Black learner, aspiring techy developer and problem solver. I am a Black organiser, contributor and cooperator. I am a Black female who is intelligent, brave, inquisitive, asks questions and likes to rave. I am different, I like a challenge and I like to learn new things. I am a Black female communicator, collaborator, excited to create new ideas and see them come to life. I am a Black female who likes to laugh and giggle but mostly enjoys the sun who comes with a diverse perspective and likes to get things done.

Because of all I am, I say why not choose me.

Why not Founders and Coders?

Founders and Coders is not your ordinary coding bootcamp. They promote diversity with no judgement for the Tech industry. Founders and Coders are making a social impact with technology all over the world. Founders and Coders are on a mission to prove that for many people peer-led learning is the most effective and least expensive route into the tech industry. Cohorts thrive when teams represent a diversity of experiences and perspectives, providing many opportunities for people to learn new ways of thinking from each other. Founders and Coders helps you learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.JS and other basic building blocks of web development, as well as user-centred design and entrepreneurship, via workshops and team projects based on ideas pitched within the cohort and for an external client. Founders and Coders teaches you to learn to code by pair programming with a partner, working on team projects, and from “near-peer” mentorship by recent graduates. Founders and Coders is a community founded on the principle of reciprocity.

Because of all Founders and Coders are, I say why not choose to learn with them.
